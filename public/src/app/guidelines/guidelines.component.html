In order to submit to a hackathon, please provide a link to your project on GitHub and a video showcasing your app on YouTube.

Video

Your video should quickly let the viewer know all about what your app does! Try to make your video as engaging as you would make a live presentation. For the judges and students at other locations who are unable to attend your live presentation, they will be relying on this video.

We request that you submit your YouTube url in the format https://youtu.be/yourVideoId. You can find this url in your video's info and settings, as shown here.

GitHub

Please provide your GitHub link! Featured here, your code will be available for other Coding Dojo students. We hope that you will take the opportunity to inspire and to be inspired. If you see something you like and think of a feature or two that could be included, fork the repo and add it in. Once you're happy with it, send a pull request to the original owner. Congratulations, you've just contributed to an open source project! If you receive a pull request, take a look at what's been sent to you and decide if it's something you would like to include in your project. Whether you choose to use the code or not, send a note to the person who sent the pull request, say hi, and let them know what you decided and why.
